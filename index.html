Frequently Asked Questions

Need help? I'm here to guide you through Echolocate!

Getting Started

How do I add my first assignment?

Click the "Add Assignment" button in the header, fill in the details, and click "Save Assignment". It's that simple!

How do I navigate the calendar?

Use the arrow buttons to move between months. Click on any day to see assignments due on that date.

Assignment Management

How do I track my progress?

Mark assignments as "In Progress" when you start working, then "Completed" when finished. You can also add grades!

Can I attach files to assignments?

Yes! When adding an assignment, you can attach multiple files using the file input field.

Study Time Tracking

How does the study timer work?

Click "Start Study Session" to begin timing. You can pause and resume as needed. When done, click "Stop" to save your session.

Can I track study time by subject?

Absolutely! When you stop a study session, you can specify which subject you were studying.

Calendar & Export

How do I export my assignments to my calendar?

Click "Export Calendar" in the header to download an .ics file that you can import into Google Calendar, Outlook, or Apple Calendar.

Will my data be saved?

Yes! All your assignments, study sessions, and preferences are saved locally in your browser.

Tips & Tricks

How can I stay organized?

Use the priority levels (High, Medium, Low) and filter assignments by subject or status to stay on top of your work!

What's the best way to use Echolocate?

Start by adding all your assignments, then use the calendar to plan your study time. Track your progress and celebrate your achievements!
